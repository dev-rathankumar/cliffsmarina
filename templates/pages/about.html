Welcome to The Cliffs Marina on Possum Kingdom Lake.

Welcome to the Cliffs Marina located on the stunning Possum Kingdom Lake. Possum Kingdom Lake is well known for being one of the most gorgeous lakes in North Texas. Whether you enjoy recreational boating or relaxing fishing, Possum Kingdom Lake provides a relaxing atmosphere to engage in water activities. While soaking up the crisp refreshing waters of Possum Kingdom Lake, donâ€™t overlook the towering cliffs that frame this beautiful body of water..

The Cliffs Marina facilitates easy access to a full day of lake fun. We offer annual leasing packages on our covered slips. Courtesy slips can also be utilized on a per availability basis.

While on the lake make sure to restock and refuel at our ship store. Inside our ship store you will find a wide variety of snacks and beverages, as well as fishing tackle supplies, so you can load up on what you need and quickly get back to having fun on the lake. Our gas stations are conveniently accessible on land or at the courtesy dock. Take advantage of our variety of watercraft rentals. Full or half-day watercraft rentals are offered with pontoon boats, kayaks, stand-up paddle boards, beach cruiser bicycles and many more. We genuinely hope you have an amazing time and take full advantage of all the services we have to offer while you visit Possum Kingdom Lake.
